How to set up your email signature

  1. Click the Copy Signature button next to your name below
  2. Open Gmail → Settings (gear icon) → See all settings → General → Signature
  3. Click into the signature editor and press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V on Mac) to paste
  4. Click Save Changes at the bottom of the page

Outlook: File → Options → Mail → Signatures → paste into the editor.
