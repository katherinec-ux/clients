SECTION 09
Co-Branding & Partner Logos
Warami-Led
Warami
Partner
Warami logo first and/or larger. Used when Warami is lead contractor.
Partner-Led
Partner
Warami
Partner logo first; Warami equal or smaller. Used as subcontractor.
Credential Strip
All logos equal size. Used for joint ventures or multi-partner bids.
Supply Nation Placement
• Always alongside the Warami logo
• Position to the right of, or below, the Warami mark
• Separated by a vertical divider line
• Never displayed larger than the Warami mark
Acceptable (Tier 1 & 2)
• Use approved colour variations only
• Proportional sizing maintained
• Minimum clear space observed
• On approved background colours
Not Acceptable
• Recoloured to match partner palette
• Placed inside a contractor frame/border
• Cropped or partially hidden
• Mark used without the wordmark
General rule: The Warami brand must remain identifiable and uncompromised in all co-branding contexts. When in doubt, consult the brand team.

Acknowledgement of Country
Acknowledgement of Country
Warami Services acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the lands on which we live, work, and build. We pay our deepest respects to Elders past, present, and emerging, and recognise the enduring connection of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples to Country, culture, and community. We are committed to walking together toward a future grounded in respect, opportunity, and shared prosperity.
